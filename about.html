Safety Policy

THS is fully committed to be an injury-free organization and safeguarding the Occupational Heatth of its direct and indirect employees at work. The Company firmly believes that safety is an integral part of its operation and all levels of management have prime resposibility towards safety. The company also firmly believes in proactive measures preventing occupational illness. In achieving these objectives,Tata Motors will involve its employees and allocate adequate resources for continous improvement in its Occupational Health and Safety performance.
